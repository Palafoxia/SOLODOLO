Photo Libraries

University Photo Library

Our photo library contains a collection of over 10,000 tagged and color-corrected photos of Princeton people, places and events. These photos may be used free of charge by University employees for University business. Photos may not be used for commercial purposes without permission. An account is required; contact our digital assets coordinator to get started. 

Public Image Resources

Separate from our collection are public photo galleries with images of the Princeton campus, as well as academic activities. These photos may be used free of charge by the University community, including alumni, by the news media and by nonprofit groups for news, educational or historic purposes. Photos may not be used for commercial purposes without permission; requests should be directed to the digital assets coordinator.

Archival Images

Requests for older images in the Princeton University Archives can be made through Seeley G. Mudd Manuscript Library.
