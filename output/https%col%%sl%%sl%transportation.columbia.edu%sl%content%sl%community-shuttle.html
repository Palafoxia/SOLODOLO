Community Shuttle

Columbia University provides free shuttle bus service for qualifying individuals via the ADA-accessible Intercampus Shuttle. The shuttle connects 96th Street, the Morningside campus, the Medical Center (CUMC), and Harlem Hospital (HH) with stops at nearby subway and transit stops.

Hours of Operation

The service runs Monday through Friday, 9:00am to 5:00pm, excluding state and federal holidays. Please review our rider etiquette guidelines.

Service Announcements

In cases of interruptions or changes in shuttle service, alerts will be displayed here on the Transportation site to keep you informed.

View and download the map and schedule

Both are available in our Community Shuttle Guide. Please note: all times are approximate.

Acceptable forms of ID

All riders must present a valid ID upon boarding the shuttle.

Medicare Health Insurance Card
Access-A-Ride ID Card
Metrocard for Senior Citizens or People with Disabilities
