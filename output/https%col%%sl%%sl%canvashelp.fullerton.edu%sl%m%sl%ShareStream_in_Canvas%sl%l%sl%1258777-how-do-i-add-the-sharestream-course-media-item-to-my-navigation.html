How do I add the ShareStream Course Media item to my navigation?

You will learn how to add the ShareStream Course Media item to your course's Navigation links in your course in Canvas.

There are basically two reasons to add this item to your navigation:

  1. It allows students to directly access all of your ShareStream videos in that course with a single click.
  2. It allows you to copy ShareStream videos from a previous course (or a Sandbox course) to your current course via the Course Media Copy Wizard.

1. Enter your course in Canvas.

2. Click on Settings.

3. Click on the Navigation tab.

4. Drag the ShareStream Course Media item into the viewable area for navigation links

5. Click on Save.

6. The ShareStream Course Media now displays in the Navigation.
