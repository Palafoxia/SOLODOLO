The CSU works diligently to help ensure one thing for our 475,000 students: the timely completion of a high quality degree in preparation for a lifetime of achievement. This vision is embodied in Graduation Initiative 2025, which sets ambitious goals for facilitating students’ paths to a college degree and eliminating equity gaps.

This analysis demonstrates the economic benefits our students will realize when we achieve our Graduation Initiative 2025 goals.
