Continuous Learning for the Defense Acquisition Workforce

Defense Acquisition Workforce members must acquire 80 Continuous Learning Points (CLPs) every two years from the date of entry into the acquisition workforce for as long as the member remains in an acquisition position per DoD Instruction 5000.66. Members are encouraged to set a goal of achieving 40 CLPs within any 12 month period.
