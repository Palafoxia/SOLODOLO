Inclusive. Inspirational. Empowering. Life-Changing.

With the graduation of the Class of 2021, the California State University achieved a milestonefour million living alumni. While 84% of CSU alumni live in California, our reach is global. Our alumni networks operate domestically in New York and Washington D.C., and new networks are forming in the United Kingdom and Tokyo.

Learn more about what our remarkable alumni are doing and find ways to get involved with your alma mater.

CSU alumni networks

New York

The CSU-NYC Alumni Network is a resource for alumni to meet industry professionals, discuss career paths and mentorship, and ask general advice about relocating to New York from California. This network encompasses not only alumni, but also parents, former instructors and friends of the CSU system.

 

Washington, D.C.

The CSU-DC Alumni Network is a social and professional network for CSU alumni living and working in the DC-MD-VA area. We host alumni mixers, educational opportunities, support CSU partners, and create a community of proud CSU graduates in the nations capital.
