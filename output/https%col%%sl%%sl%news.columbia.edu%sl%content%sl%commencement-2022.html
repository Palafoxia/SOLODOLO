Commencement 2022

Commencement 2022 Stories

It's that time of year. Banners are being hung, bleachers are being installed, and our pride in the Class of 2022 is reaching a fever pitch that is all the more palpable since it has been three long years since we've celebrated an in-person Commencement.

On this page, you'll find stories from graduates, photos of the season, trivia, and the latest updates on what to expect from this year's Commencement and various graduation celebrations. You'll also find content celebrating our Classes of 2021 and 2020 who have been invited back to campus for a special ceremony since their in-person Commencement festivities were canceled due to COVID-19.

If you're looking for logistical information, the schedule of ceremonies, and more, please click the button below to visit the full Commencement website. You can find COVID-19 policies for Commencement here. 

Congratulations, graduates! #RoarLions2022.
