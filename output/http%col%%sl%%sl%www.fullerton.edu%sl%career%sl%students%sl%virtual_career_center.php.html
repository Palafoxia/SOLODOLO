Scheduled Apppointments:

You can schedule a Virtual Career Counseling appointment Online through Titan Connection!

Virtually meet with a College Career Specialist or Career Coach on various topics including: graduate school, interview preparation, major/career exploration and many more. 

Schedule your appointment now using Titan Connection.

Note: When logging into Titan Connection, please select Calendar - Counseling Appointment from the left hand column and select, "New Appointment".  


NOTE: Effective May 31, 2022, Scheduled Appointments (In-Person & Virtual) for College of HHD and HSS will be offered through Titan Net, which you can access through your Student Portal. Appointments for these two colleges will not be offered through Titan Connection.

Access Titan Net

Drive-Thru Appointments:

No time for an appointment? No Problem, Please stop by for a quick 10 to 15 minute Resume/Cover Letter Review or Job Search.

Instructions:

  • Select the "Schedule Appointment" button below to login to Titan Connection
  • On the Home Page, on the left-hand menu, select "Counseling Appointment"
  • Select Drive-Thru Button
  • You will be placed in a Queue (Waiting Room)
  • When a Counselor is available, a Zoom Link will be provided for you to join a meeting (Zoom Link will appear on your screen within Titan Connection

*NOTE: Drive-Thru Appointments are not intended for Academic Advising. Drive-Thru is on a first come, first serve basis. Please be patient as you wait in the Waiting Room for a Counselor to attend to you.

VMock: Your New Virtual Resume Assistant

Resumes are a vital part of you landing your dream job. To get that standout resume, you now have access to a powerful and easy-to-use automated online resume review platform from VMock, your Virtual Resume Assistant.

Get instant feedback from VMock right after submitting your resume. Analyze your resume anytime, anywhere, 24/7. So no need to wait for someone to have your resume reviewed.

Using VMock is as simple as:

  1. Log in
  2. Upload your resume
  3. Get real-time analysis
  4. Review the results
  5. Make changes based on the analysis

 

Play the Video below to see how VMock Works:

Utilizing powerful machine learning algorithms and artificial intelligence, your resume gets benchmarked against thousands of resumes from top institutions from around the world. The software automatically analyzes your resume based on presentation, impact and skills and lets you know what areas you need to improve and how to do so.

 

So don't let your dream job or internship pass you up. VMock is completely free for all Titans to use. So let VMock help you get that perfect resume!

 Assessments are tools that identify various areas including interests, values, skills and personality. Through this process students can begin to explore majors, jobs and career choices that are most appropriate for them.

 

Strong Interest Inventory

The Strong Interest Inventory is an assessment that will help students match their interests with potential majors and careers. 

    • $15 due before assessment review
    • Assesses interests, revealing patterns
    • Shows how interest patterns relate to occupations

To Take the Strong Interest Inventory

    1. Schedule your Major/Career Exploration Phase I with a Career Specialist.
    2. Take the Online Assessment(s).
    3. The Career Center will provide you with a link to the Assessment via email after you have completed your pre-assessment appointment.
    4. Schedule your Assessment Results Review with a Career Specialist.

Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)

The MBTI is an assessment that can provide insight into how your personality compares to others and your work preferences. Research shows that personality traits play a major role in job satisfaction and success. 

    • $15 due before assessment review
    • Assesses personality
    • Helps to better understand the relationship between preferences and an ideal work environment

 To Take the MBTI

    1. Schedule your Major/Career Exploration Phase I with a Career Development Specialist.
    2. Take the Online Assessment(s).
    3. The Career Center will provide you with a link to the Assessment via email after you have completed your pre-assessment appointment.
    4. Schedule your Assessment Results Review with the Career Development Specialist.
