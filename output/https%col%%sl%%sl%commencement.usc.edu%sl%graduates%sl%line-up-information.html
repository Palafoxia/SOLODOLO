Assembly and Procession

Graduating students may start to line up for the processional into Alumni Park beginning at 7:30 a.m. The processional will begin promptly at 8:30 a.m.

Graduating students will process in and sit with other students from their school/program. The map below denotes the lineup locations and processional paths. Students should refer to the listing for their school/programs line up location.

Please note that the stage is in front of Doheny Library. Immediate seating in the front sections are reserved for processing members of the Class of 2022.

Honor Cords


Undergraduate students graduating with a GPA of 3.5 or higher are eligible to wear honor cords. Schools will notify eligible students via USC e-mail with details.

Cap and Tassel Protocol

Caps should be worn at all times, except during the invocation, benediction, and National Anthem, when gentlemen should remove their caps. The tassel is worn over the right temple. When the President salutes the graduates, it is appropriate for the graduates to respond by tipping their caps. Graduates may then move their tassels over to the left side.
