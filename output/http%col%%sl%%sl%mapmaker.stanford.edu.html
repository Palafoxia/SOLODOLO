Custom Map Maker

Get started with the Stanford Custom Map Maker

Creating your custom map is as easy as 1, 2, 3...

  1. Select My Maps → Create new Map
  2. Choose the datasets you want displayed
  3. Customize with your feature and labels, and Save!

After creating your custom map, here is what you can do:

  • Share your map with others for further collaboration
  • Generate a PDF image of your own map for printing and distribution
  • Embed powerful interactive maps on your own website in minutes

The intended audience is authorized Stanford staff. Please contact us at online_mapping@lists.stanford.edu for additional information.
