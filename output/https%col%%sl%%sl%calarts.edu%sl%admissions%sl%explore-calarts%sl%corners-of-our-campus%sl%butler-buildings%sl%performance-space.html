Butler Buildings

A set of smaller outlying buildings, the Butler Buildings hold spaces for stop motion, film production, theater, and creative writing.

Performance Space

A larger multi-use studio/performance space. Approximately 1,500 square feet, BB2 has a truss and pipe grid, lighting inventory, dimming, sound inventory, and a flexible seating riser system that can be brought in for performances.
