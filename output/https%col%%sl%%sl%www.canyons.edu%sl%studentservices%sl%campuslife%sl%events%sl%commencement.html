Commencement 2022

Home > Student Services > Campus Life & Student Engagement > Campus Events > Commencement Ceremony

June 2 or June 3, 2022

Congratulations to our graduating students who have persisted and completed their educational goals during these challenging times! Commencement is a special occasion that graduates, families, guests, and the campus community look forward to celebrating each year. We are excited to announce that College of the Canyons will offer two options that will allow graduates the opportunity to choose the graduation format that works best for them and their guests. In order to accommodate as many graduates in as safe a manner as possible, those wishing to participate in this year’s graduation festivities may select ONE of the following graduation ceremony formats to participate in.

  • Grad Walk is a walk-through graduation which provides an opportunity for graduates to wear their cap and gown while walking across the graduation stage with their guests.  Each graduate will have their name read and a picture taken by a professional photographer.  The Grad Walk is a walk-through experience that is estimated to last 30 minutes from start to finish. Graduates will be allowed a maxium of 6 guests

    Grad Walk will be held on Thursday, June 2, 2022 in the Honor Grove with 30 minute time slots available between 3:00pm-7:00pm.

  • For those who do not wish or are unable to participate in the Grad Walk, College of the Canyons will also hold a traditional seated ceremony on Friday, June 3, 2022 in the Honor Grove at 8:00am.  Graduates will need to check-in no later than 6:45am dressed in their cap & gown.  The traditional graduation ceremony is estimated to last 2-3 hours and conclude by 11:00am.  Graduates will be allowed a maxium of 2 guests

We sincerely hope you decide to reward yourself and acknowledge your academic accomplishments by attending one of the graduation celebrations.

You may watch a livestream broadcast of the Traditional Graduation Ceremony, starting Friday, June 3, 2022 at 8:00 am.

    • Grad Walk CEREMONY Reservations (6 guests max allowed):
      • Grad Walk reservations will be available beginning Monday, May 2, 2022.  Please make sure to check your student email in the next few weeks for additional information about reservations including a link to the online reservation form.
      • Reservations for graduates and guests are required.  Those without a reservation will not be admitted to the Grad Walk.
      • In order to accommodate all of the graduates, graduates may sign-up for ONE 30 minute Grad Walk available time slot.
      • Graduates may not reserve more than one Grad Walk timeslot.
      • Please note there are a limited amount of graduates who may sign up for each timeslot.
      • Grad Walk reservations may not be exchanged for participation in the traditional graduation ceremony.
      • Graduates who participate in the Grad Walk will be permitted to bring a maximum of 6 guests.
      • Reservations will be accepted until 6:00pm on Thursday, May 19, 2022.  No reservations will be accepted after this date.
      • Due to space limitations and maximum capacity restrictions, reservations will be issued on a first-come first-serve basis.
    • Traditional Graduation Ceremony Reservations (2 GUESTS MAX ALLOWED):
      • Traditional graduation ceremony reservations will be available beginning Monday, May 2, 2022.  Please make sure to check your student email in the next few weeks for additional information about reservations including a link to the online reservation form.
      • Reservations for graduates and guests are required.  Those without a reservation will not be admitted to the graduation ceremony.
      • Traditional graduation ceremony reservations may not be exchanged for participation in the Grad Walk.
      • Graduates who participate in the traditional graduation ceremony will be permitted to bring a maximum of 2 guests.
      • Due to space limitations and for the safety of graduates, guests, and members of the campus community, additional guests will NOT be permitted under any circumstance.
      • Reservations will be accepted until 6:00pm on Thursday, May 19, 2022. No reservations will be accepted after this date.
      • Due to space limitations and maximum capacity restrictions, reservations will be issued on a first-come first-serve basis.
