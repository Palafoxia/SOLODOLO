World-Class Colleges and Majors

Our dedicated, friendly, and award-winning faculty will help you look further, reach higher, and think deeper. Select a major from the list below to learn more about what our seven colleges and schools have to offer you.

Gain the innovative training needed to become a leading professional in your field.

Majors include:

  • Bioengineering
  • Computer Science
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering

College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences (CHASS)

Be "at home in the world," exploring human meaning in social and historical contexts.

Majors include:

  • Creative Writing
  • Economics
  • Global Studies
  • Psychology

Learn More

College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences (CNAS)

Improve the quality of life for those living in your community and around the world.

Majors include:

  • Neuroscience
  • Physics

Learn how to address and solve the business challenges of the 21st century.

  • Marketing

Campus Security and Fire Safety Report

The Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act (Clery Act) requires UCR (as an institution of higher education receiving federal financial aid) to report specified crime statistics on college campuses and to provide other safety and crime information to all current and prospective students and employees. The Annual Security and Fire Safety Report is available on the Chief Compliance Office website. If you would like a paper copy of the Report, you may contact the Clery Coordinator via email at clery@ucr.edu or by phone at (951) 827-6223.
