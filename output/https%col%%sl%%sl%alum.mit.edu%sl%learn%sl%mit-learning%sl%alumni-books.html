Alumni Books

Alumni Book Club on Goodreads

Since September 2016, MIT faculty have led a monthly Goodreads discussion forum about books they love.

Alumni-Authored Books

Read any good books lately? There is a strong chance one of them may have been authored by one of your fellow MIT alumni. Check out the Alumni Associations ever-growing list of MIT Alumni books (PDF) to find out. You can use this list to inform your own reading or your book club selections, or to connect with MIT authors.

What are you reading now? Help the Association update the list with other MIT alumni-authored books.  

Alumni Books Podcast

Tune in to the Alumni Books Podcast to hear interviews with alumni authors. Available on Soundcloud and iTunes.
