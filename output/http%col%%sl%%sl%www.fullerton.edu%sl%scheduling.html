The mission of the Scheduling Office is to support California State University, Fullerton through the effective use of facilties for:

  • Academic and Instructional classes
  • University-related event reservations

The office is a resource for data collection and reporting to assist in the planning of space utilization and enrollment management.

FACility priority

  1. Academic instruction supported by the state receive the first priority for all facilities on campus.
  2. Second priority goes to other regular academic activities. This category includes regular lecture classes and labs, exams and other instructional activity.
  3. Non-state sponsored events may be reassigned to a new location if an academic priority emerges. Exceptions will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
