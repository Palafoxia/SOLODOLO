Visitor Health and Safety Policies

All performance attendees to Juilliard are required to be fully vaccinated against COVID-19. 

Individuals are considered fully vaccinated two weeks after receiving the final dose of a vaccine authorized by the World Health Organization or the Food and Drug Administration. 

Please be aware that those not currently eligible for vaccination, including children under 5 years of age, are not permitted to attend Juilliard performances at this time. Children between 5 and 11 years of age who are partially vaccinatedi.e. two weeks or more after their first dosemay attend Juilliard performances WHEN ACCOMPANIED BY A FULLY VACCINATED ADULT. Please see below for more information.

Proof of vaccination can be shown via any of the following free mobile apps: 

  • CLEAR Health Pass (Apple or Android). This will be the fastest method to enter the performance venues. Choose the venue name of the performance location. 

  • New York State Excelsior Pass 

  • New York City COVID Safe App (Apple or Android) 

PLAN AHEAD! Please download one of these apps and upload your vaccination card/information before arriving at Juilliard.  

If you are unable to use one of the apps, you may present a paper vaccination card together with a government-issued ID to a member of our Front-of-House staff.  

All visitors are required to wear appropriate face coverings while visiting Juilliard School or Lincoln Center facilities. For information on proper mask usage, please refer to CDC guidelines. 

If you feel sick, have been exposed to COVID-19, or have been ordered by a public health authority to quarantine or isolate, please do not enter any Juilliard facility until medically cleared. We also ask that you refrain from visiting campus if you, or anyone in your household, has had a fever, flu-like symptoms, or any COVID-19 symptoms within the previous 14 days. 

These policies and guidelines will continue to evolve and are subject to change. Bookmark this page for the latest guidelines. 

Bringing a Minor to a performance? 

You can use any of the above proof of vaccination options. Please follow the below steps to set up a minor with CLEAR Health Pass:

  1. Create your won CLEAR account and Health pass

  2. Click the Account icon on the bottom right hand corner

  3. Select "Minor Accounts"

  4. Add in your child's email address, set up their account, and have your child snap a selfie

Here are some helpful enrollment hints! Once you have set up an account for your child, all they need to do is:

  • Download CLEAR on your smartphone

  • Log in with the email and password the parent or guardian set up

  • Click on the white Health Pass tile and enter the code Juilliard

  • Add a vaccine card and have your green pass ready!

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the box office hours? 

 

You can reach the Juilliard Box Office at (212) 769-7406 (Tuesday-Thursday, 2-6pm) or at [email protected] (Monday-Friday, 10am-6pm). The Box Office walk-up window is open 2 hours before all ticketed performances and Tuesday-Thursday, 2-6pm.

 

2. How can I contact the box office? 

The box office can be reached via phone at (212) 769-7406 or via email at [email protected]. 

 

3. E-Ticket Information 

An electronic ticket (e-ticket) can be presented via smart device or printed at home for entry access. The e-ticket(s) will be emailed to you at the time of purchase. You can also access your e-tickets through your account at any time. 

 

4. Refunds/ Exchanges 

All sales are final. Refunds are only allowed in limited circumstances, as explained in the Canceled, rescheduled, or postponed performance policy section. 

 

5. Cant use your tickets? Heres how to donate them. 

To donate tickets you cannot use, please contact the box office at [email protected] or (212) 769-7406. 

 

6. Juilliard student/faculty/staff ticket information 

Juilliard students, faculty, and staff may obtain their complimentary and discounted tickets by logging into the Spektrix ticketing system via Juilliards Performance Calendar or by visiting juilliard.edu/account and using their Juilliard credentials. 

 

7. Non-Juilliard student ticket policy 

Non-Juilliard students may purchase student-priced tickets with proof of valid full-time student status. Please email [email protected] to set up a student account. 

 

8. Military discount information 

Military veterans and active-duty military personnel may purchase discounted tickets. Proof of status is required. Email [email protected] for account setup. 

 

9. Group ticket information 

Groups of 10 or more may receive discounted tickets to the event if available provided all tickets are purchased together. Payments are due at the time of reservation. Groups may be seated in any section of the venue, pending ticket availability. Discounts will be applied automatically in the cart of the person purchasing all tickets. Contact the Juilliard box office at (212) 769-7406 or [email protected] for more information. 

 

11. Accessibility Information 

Accessible seats are designated spaces reserved for patrons with accessibility needs or disabilities. There are no fixed seats in these locations and the adjacent seat is allocated for a companion. Use by nondisabled persons is prohibited and may result in forfeiture of tickets and any associated costs.  

For further information or assistance, please contact the box office at (212) 769-7406 or [email protected]. 

 

12. Different types of ticketed events 

a.     Free, no tickets required  

b.     Free, tickets required 

c.     Paid, tickets required 

 

13. Livestreamed and on-demand performances 

Many Juilliard performances are also available via livestream at juilliard.edu/calendar. You can filter for the Live Streaming performance type. Performances that are available on demand can be found in Juilliards media gallery.  

 

14. Tickets to Juilliard events in other venues 

Tickets for Juilliard concerts in Alice Tully Hall, David Geffen Hall, and Carnegie Hall, and any other outside venues may be secured at the box offices located at the respective venues. The Juilliard performance calendar provides information and a link to those box offices. 

 

15. Code of conduct 

Patrons must comply with the venues applicable rules, policies, terms, and conditions. The venue reserves the right, without refund, to refuse admission to or eject any person whose conduct management deems disorderly, who uses vulgar or abusive language, or who fails to comply with venue rules. 

 

16. What to do if a performance is sold out 

If a performance is sold out, please continue to check online to see if tickets become available as we get closer to performances. Juilliard does not have a standby line or a waiting list for sold-out performances. 

 

17. Canceled, rescheduled, or postponed performance policy 

If a performance is canceled, rescheduled, or postponed, Juilliard may at its discretion refund the tickets, or patrons can donate their tickets to help support Juilliards educational mission. 

 

18. How to get to Juilliard 

Please see juilliard.edu/directions for more information on public transportation to Juilliard and parking options. 

 

19. How can I become a member? 

When you become a member of Juilliard, you not only receive perks that will enlighten your life, but you also support the next generation of performing artists. Memberships begin at $100 per year. Learn more. 

 

20. House management information 

a.     Age restrictions 

Children under the age of 5 are not permitted in Juilliards theaters. 

b.     Recording 

All photography and video/audio recordings of events are strictly prohibited. You may not record, copy, publicly exhibit, transmit, or distribute any virtual event through any means; resell views of any virtual event; or allow others to log into your account for the purpose of watching a virtual event. 

c.     Juilliard venues 

Learn more about Juilliard venues. 

d.     Content and production warnings 

Juilliard uses reasonable efforts to relay content and production warnings before the performance whenever possible.   

e.     Bag/coat check 

All ticket holders and their belongings may be subject to search when entering the venues. Any item that the organizer or venue security personnel considers a security risk or that may be used to damage the venue or interfere with the event will be confiscated. 

d.     Late seating 

Late seating is not permitted except at the discretion of management. 

e.     Lost and found 

If you have misplaced an item before, during or after a performance, you may report it by calling (212) 799-5000. 

f.      Fire safety regulations 

Fire safety regulations require that aisles are kept clear of obstruction, including mobility assistive devices. 

g.     Food and beverages 

No food or beverages are allowed in Juilliard venues.
