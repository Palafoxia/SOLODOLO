Staff Development

Opportunities Abound

The Office of the Vice President for Student Affairs offers countless trainings and professional development opportunities for professional and personal growth of Division of SA employees. A primary reason for hosting these various learning opportunities is out of care for the students. Our belief is that we must always continue to learn and improve so that we can best support student success. In collaboration with the SA Leadership Team and other educators on and off campus, staff members have the chance to further develop their communication skills, technological proficiency, networking, and health and wellness, among other topics.
