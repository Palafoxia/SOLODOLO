Hardware Recommendations

These hardware recommendations are provided by University IT for Stanford University faculty and staff purchases.

Contact University IT for unique hardware requirements

This website will help you select a computer or printer system to meet your personal and departmental needs.

Laptop, Desktop and Tablet Recommendations

The fastest processors available and enough RAM and storage to easily keep up with the latest software releases over the next three years. *Some tablets are not recommended as primary computers. Please see the recommendations for for more details. Recommended models are suitable for:

  • MS Office, web browsing, email
  • Databases, statistics, programming
  • Photo, music, and PDF editing, web development, and much more!

Flexible Open Floor Plans, Telecommuting, and Remote Work

Moving from a private office to a flexible open floor plan or telecommuting requires more than just a change in how you work. It can also mean modifying or adding to your existing office equipment to accommodate the unique circumstances of your new work environment.

Computer Softphone and Video Conferencing Accessories

University IT offers softphone and video conferencing options that you can use to meet and collaborate with colleagues across campus or around the world, reducing travel time and expense while increasing communication and sharing.

Printer Recommendations

The Cardinal Print Managed Print Services (MPS) program for Stanford University lets you use your SUNet ID and password to securely print, copy, or scan a document using a vendor-managed multifunction device. You can also manage your workflow at the printer or online, and send faxes using the Cardinal Fax email faxing service. If you have questions about the program, please email the Cardinal Print team.

Learn more
