Driving Directions

The UC Riverside Student Recreation Center is located on the north end of campus, off of W. Linden Street near Canyon Crest Drive. From Highway 60, exit University Avenue and turn left (east) onto University Avenue and turn left onto Canyon Crest Drive. Our building is located behind the police station, next to the track field across from A & I Residence Halls.

Click here for Google driving directions.

Parking

SRC parking is available in any guest lot (i.e. 24, 25, 30, and 22). Parking lot guest passes may also be purchased at the Parking Kiosk at the UCR entrance off of University Ave., and on Canyon Crest Drive off of Martin Luther King Blvd. Please visit the Transportation Services for additional parking information.
