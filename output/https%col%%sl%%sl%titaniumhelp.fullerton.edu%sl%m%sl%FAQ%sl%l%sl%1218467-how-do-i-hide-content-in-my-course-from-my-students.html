How do I hide content in my course from my students?

You will learn how to hide content on your course main page, so that your students cannot see it.

1. Enter your course in TITANium.

2. Click on the Gear icon.

3. Select Turn editing on.

4. Scroll down the page and click on Edit.

Note: All of these items are currently visible to students.

5. Select Hide.

6. The item is now hidden.

Note the "Hidden from students" notice for you. Students will not see the item at all.

Student view of Week 1.

Note that students do not see that PowerPoint at all.

This technique will work with all types of files, activities, and resources. You can hide them completely from the students.
