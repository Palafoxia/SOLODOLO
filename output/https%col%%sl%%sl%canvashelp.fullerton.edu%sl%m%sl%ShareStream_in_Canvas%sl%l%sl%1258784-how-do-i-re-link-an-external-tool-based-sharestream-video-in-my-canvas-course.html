How do I re-link an External Tool-based ShareStream video in my Canvas course?

You will learn how to re-link a ShareStream video that was copied from a course in TITANium. Generally, speaking, External Tool link ShareStream videos will not need to be re-linked. That is one of the benefits of having used the External tool link in TITANium.

1. Make sure that you are not logged in to the version of TITANium where the course was copied.

2. Enter your course in Canvas.

3. Click on the External tool link.

Does your video display and run?
Yes. The video displays.

Verify that you are not logged in to TITANium.

4. Click on the Play button to be certain the video plays.

Since the video works, you do not need to re-link it. You're good to go>

How do I verify that this video is in my Canvas course?

There are two ways to check which ShareStream videos are currently located in your course.

I want to use the Pick-n-Play.

Follow this guide to learn how to view the contents of the ShareStream Pick-n-Play.

I want to use the MediaManager.

To verify which videos and to copy specific videos to your course, you can use the ShareStream Media Manager.

Follow this guide to learn how to use the MediaManager.

1. Click on the Modules link.

2. Click on the three dots menu.

3. Select Remove.

4. The External link has now been deleted.
