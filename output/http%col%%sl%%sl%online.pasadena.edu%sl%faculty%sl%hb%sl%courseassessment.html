Teaching online, hybrid, or web-enhanced courses opens new possibilities for classroom innovation and creativity. While there are many similarities between teaching an online class and a traditional one, distance education requires alternative methods for engaging students and delivering course instruction. Whether this is your first time teaching distance education (online/hybrid) or you are well-experienced in it, we are here to support you in your training and instruction of online-based courses!
