Emsi

Emsi (Economic Modeling Specialists Intl.) provides labor market data covering more than 99% of the workforce, compiled from a wide variety of government sources, job postings, and online profile and resumes.  The Office of Assessment and Institutional Effectiveness has access to Emsi products, including Alumni Outcomes and Analyst.  Alumni Outcomes provides labor market data compiled from various sources matched to a CSUF database of student records.  Analyst provides demographic, economic, and occupational trends and outlook data locally and nationally.

CSUF employees can use the Tableau Enterprise Login to access the Workforce Outcomes dashboard, which is located in the Alumni Outcomes folder. 

For more information on using the dashboard, please refer to the Alumni Workforce Outcomes Dashboard Guide.
