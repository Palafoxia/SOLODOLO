On this page

Join us to meet student leaders and get acquainted with service and civic engagement at Princeton University. Start at Welcome to Service to enjoy refreshments outside Dillon Gym from 11 a.m. to 12 p.m. then head under the tent from 12 to 3 p.m. to meet service and civic engagement groups at Involvement Fest.
