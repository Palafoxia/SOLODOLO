Contact

We are a collection of Fire and Natural Resource Advisers with a mission to deliver information and support for communities throughout California. Please feel free to contact us with questions about fire-related topics. 

If you have questions about current fire activity or if you feel that you are at risk from fire, please contact your local authorities for information and updates. 

Workgroup: Fire

Researches wildland fire mitigation and wildland fire use including WUI fire impacts, fire effects research, decision support tools and helping communities with planning in the WUI.
