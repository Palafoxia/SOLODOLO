How do I log in to TITANium?

You will learn how to log in to the CSUF Portal and then into TITANium to see your list of courses.

1. Go to my.fullerton.edu.

Type my.fullerton.edu in your web browser address and hit Enter.

2. Sign into the Portal.

  1. Type your username.
  2. Type your password.
  3. Click the Sign In button.

Please contact your department if you do not have a username and password to access the CSUF Portal.

3. Three Ways to go to TITANium.

Click on the TITANium Faculty link.

If you want to navigate directly to a course, click on the link within the My Courses block.
If you don't see the TITANIUM FACULTY link, type TITANium in the search field.

Then, click on the TITANium link when it appears.

4. Click on the TITANium button for the current term

The TITANium buttons at the top of the page link to the current Academic Year courses and the TITANium Communities site.

Links to courses in prior academic periods are listed at the bottom of this page.

5. Dashboard

In a new window, you will see the Dashboard page where your courses are listed. Click on a course title or image to enter a course.
