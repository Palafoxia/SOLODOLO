Drop-In Advising Sessions

Have a quick question? 15-minute drop-in sessions are available in-person and virtually every Tuesday through Thursday, 11:00 am - 2:00 pm. Visit the Career Center (Strathmore, 3rd Floor) or the virtual drop-in room for resume/cover letter reviews, to seek help with your job/internship search, or interview preparation.

*On Thursday, June 16th, Virtual Drop-in sessions will not be available. We apologize for the inconvenience. 

Access Virtual Drop-In Sessions

Reserve an Interview Room at the Career Center

Need a quiet space to conduct your virtual interview or your virtual coaching appointment? The UCLA Career Center has interview rooms available on the 3rd Floor of Strathmore that students can reserve for virtual interviews or appointments. Space is limited and room reservations are only available during Career Center office hours: 9am-5pm.

Learn More About Career Ready Bootcamp

Get a head start on your career by enrolling in the Early Career Engagement Certificate Program (ECEC) designed especially for first year and transfer students.

The UCDC Summer in Washington Program offers Bruins a ten-week Summer internship in Washington DC where you’ll build skills and grow your professional network.
