Download text data on UC Berkeley COVID-19 cases and testing (CSV) The following dashboard provides information on COVID-19 testing at University Health Services. It provides a look at new cases and trends, at a glance. The data is updated each morning by 6 a.m. If disturbing trends or developments occur, the campus will, in addition, share that information with the campus community via posts on this web site or other campus communications. The dashboard shows the number of positive cases for tests performed at UHS. It does not include saliva tests that were performed through the Innovative Genomics Institute’s FAST Study. Visit the City of Berkeley COVID-19 Dashboard and Alameda County COVID-19 Dashboard for more information about COVID testing, cases, and vaccination rates in the community.
