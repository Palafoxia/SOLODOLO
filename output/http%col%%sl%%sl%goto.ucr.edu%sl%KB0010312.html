Goto Short Links are no longer available

The Goto Short Links service is no longer available and has been shut down as of February 10, 2022. 

If your Google Workspace domain has Short Links configured, they will no longer redirect.

Google Workspace Domain Administrators can alternatively, use open-source alternatives and third-party vendors that provide link shortening functionality, such as the GoLinks add-on for Google Docs, available through the Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) Marketplace.
