International Internships

  • 100x100 Net

    Region: Europe
    Country: Spain
    Field of Interest: Journalism and Mass Communication, Marketing, Advertising, and PR
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    100x100 Net works in the digitalization of the online world. They have assisted large companies and starups in online strategy & marketing, design & development, online broadcasting & communication, and IT management. The intern will work in marketing, social media and digital communication, as well as strategic communications. This internship is offered in collaboration with The Institute of American Universities (IAU).

  • 1516 Green Energy Technology Company Limited

    Region: Asia
    Country: Vietnam
    Field of Interest: Community Engagement & Advocacy, Ecology and Environment, Engineering (ALL), Marketing, Advertising, and PR
    Type of Organization: Non-profit / NGO

    1516 Green Energy (1516GE) is a Vietnam based company founded in 2015 by Le Vu Cuong with the aim to provide basic products and services to people living in remote and rural areas in Vietnam. The first product developed by 1516GE was a portable wind turbine and solar system that enables people in those rural areas to access electricity, and therefore, improve their living conditions. IIP interns will work with engineers and project leads to understand and contribute to 1516's ongoing projects.

  • 936 Studio

    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Argentina
    Field of Interest: Business and Consulting, Computer Science, Engineering (ALL), Graphic Design
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    936 Studio is a creative advertising and design agency based in Mendoza, Argentina.  The studio works to use audiovisual tools in an innovative way to strengthen small and medium enterprises, to give life to entrepreneurs, and to empower NGOs.  936 Studio works in four main areas: creation of audiovisual content (primarily photos and videos), brand development, website creation and enhancement, and digital marketing.  The intern will have the chance to work alongside the founders as a member of their dynamic team in graphic and web design. This internship is offered in collaboration with Puentes Abroad.

  • A*STAR, Institute of Medical Biology (IMB)

    Region: Asia
    Country: Singapore
    Field of Interest: Biological Sciences, Engineering (ALL), Health Fields
    Type of Organization: Academic Institution / Lab Research

    As the newest institute of the A*STAR biomedical sciences cluster, IMB focuses its research portfolio on the critical and challenging interface between basic science and medicine. IMBs goal is to understand mechanisms underlying human disease, in order to discover new and effective approaches to combating illness and promoting wellbeing. IIP interns will be placed in a lab which is tasked with developing efficient protocols that convert pluripotential hESCs into functional beta cells. Interns will be paired with a senior member of the lab as they pursue one of the labs fast-moving projects. If you are enrolled in the Global Health Program certificate, this internship will fulfill the GHP internship requirement.

  • Academy of Sciences, Czech Republic

    Region: Europe
    Country: Czech Republic
    Field of Interest: Biological Sciences, Physical Sciences
    Type of Organization: Academic Institution / Lab Research

    The Center for Nanobiology and Structural Biology is a joint laboratory between the Academy of Sciences and the University of South Bohemia. With its focus on molecular systems biology, the relationship between structure and function of proteins, dynamic changes related to functional processes on the level of proteins, and the mutual interaction of co-factors and sub-units in protein complexes, the laboratory is an integral part of the new research of the Institute. Interns will use specialized software to model biological systems and carry out molecular dynamics simulations. Each intern will be responsible for a sub-project and will have the opportunity to present their work at a Symposium on Structure Systems Biology.

  • Accelerate Search Limited

    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Argentina
    Field of Interest: Business and Consulting, Journalism and Mass Communication
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    Accelerate is an international executive recruitment firm, with an expert team dedicated to finding their clients top quality talent by using a direct headhunting search model. The company operates on a global basis and is dedicated to tailoring its service to match specific clients needs.  IIP interns have the opportunity to work alongside the co-founders and directors on the communications and business development teams, while truly serving as vital team members of this dynamic and growing company. This internship is offered in collaboration with Puentes Abroad.

  • ACN Worldwide

    Region: Asia
    Country: China
    Field of Interest: Business and Consulting, Finance
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    ACN Worldwide is a multi-dimensional and multi-functional investment service and trading platform dedicated to being a resource for its clients, investors, partners, and other interested parties in order to help them better understand the importance of spreading risks in their investments and appreciate the diverse opportunities available in different parts of the world. IIP interns will work on at least two designated projects to develop both Teaser and Offering Summaries, and will be involved in research and development of ACN Worldwide Country Fact Books. This placement is offered in collaboration with Absolute Internship. 

  • Agro-Tourism Association

    Region: Africa
    Country: Uganda
    Field of Interest: Business and Consulting, Community Engagement & Advocacy, Ecology and Environment, Food and Nutrition, Journalism and Mass Communication, Marketing, Advertising, and PR
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    Agro-Tourism Association is an agriculture and tourism development company that aims to promote agribusiness as a catalyst for sustainable incomes. The organizations offers a range of services designed to enhance capacity farmers to engage in farming as a profitable business. IIP interns will work as a research assistant, a marketing officer, or a business development officer, depending on interest and skill set.

  • aidha

    Region: Asia
    Country: Singapore
    Field of Interest: Community Engagement & Advocacy, Gender and Sexuality, Law and Public Policy
    Type of Organization: Non-profit / NGO

    Aidha is an award winning Singapore-registered charity. Its mission is to provide financial literacy and self-development skills to foreign domestic workers and lower income women to enable them and their families to break out of the cycle of poverty. Aidha is a small but successful organization, now in an exciting stage of growth and scaling up of its operations. Since its inception, Aidha has impacted more than 4,500 women and their families and communities. IIP interns will work on projects related to programming, marketing, and communications.

  • Alquilando

    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Argentina
    Field of Interest: Business and Consulting, Finance, Marketing, Advertising, and PR
    Type of Organization: Start-up

    Alquilando is a digital ecosystem for housing rentals that connects property owners, renters, and real estate agents in order to provide a rental experience that is safer, more practical, and online. Similar to Airbnb, Alquilando has improved and modernized renting, but instead of short-term or holiday bookings, Alquilando specializes in long-term rental contracts. Interns will be mentored by the co-founders of the company, and have the opportunity to become immersed in the real estate industry / tech startup sector. This internship is offered in collaboration with Puentes Abroad.

  • Amadema

    Region: Europe
    Country: Cyprus
    Field of Interest: Engineering (ALL)
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    AmaDema is an SME company based in the sunny island of Cyprus, in the Eastern Mediterranean Sea that focuses on the enhancement of technical fabrics and prepregs to be used in fiber reinforced composites. The interns will have the choice to work in various projects: in designing experimental procedures using available and outsourced technical equipment, in performing experimental testing using the available equipment, in manufacturing process operation and set up, and or contribute in operate and maintenance of technical equipment. This placement is offered in collaboration with Empiria Learning.

  • American Chamber of Commerce in Taiwan

    Region: Asia
    Country: Taiwan
    Field of Interest: Community Engagement & Advocacy, Government, Journalism and Mass Communication
    Type of Organization: Government

    The American Chamber of Commerce in Taiwan is dedicated to promoting the interests of international business in Taiwan. AmCham has become one of the largest, most effective business organizations in Taiwan. With approximately 1,000 members representing more than 500 companies, AmCham speaks with collective authority on international business matters in every sector of the economy. Interns will join the reporting staff of AmChams business magazine, and will research and write articles for publication, as well as assist in the editorial process and gain exposure to the world of international business.

  • Antoniou McCollum & Co

    Region: Europe
    Country: Cyprus
    Field of Interest: Law and Public Policy
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    Antoniou McCollum & Co. is a boutique law practice based in Nicosia.  The firm is ranked as top-tier firm by The Legal 500 and specialises in commercial, corporate, competition and regulatory matters. Interns will be engaged in assisting practitioners of all levels with legal research, reviewing contractual and other legal documentation. This placement is offered in collaboration with Empiria Learning.

  • ARDOR Group

    Region: Asia
    Country: Vietnam
    Field of Interest: Architecture, Business and Consulting, Engineering (ALL), Graphic Design
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    ARDOR Group designs high quality buildings and leads research for new building construction technology and materials with sustainable design and environmentally friendly standards. The team provides top quality consultancy services for design, urban planning, architectural design, structural design, and mechanical and electrical design for residential and industrial building. The IIP intern would join the building consultancy design services team and work on projects related to business development and partnership communications. This internship is offered in collaboration with ABROADER.

  • Arepita

    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Argentina
    Field of Interest: Journalism and Mass Communication, Marketing, Advertising, and PR
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    Arepita is the only daily email newsletter that curates and creatively repackages substantial news about Venezuela and seeks to create a community that uses journalism and creativity to understand what is happening in Venezuela. Interns will analyze the company's existing social media communications, research best practices, and propose update strategies. This placement is offered in collaboration with Puentes Abroad.

  • ArQuants

    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Argentina
    Field of Interest: Business and Consulting, Computer Science, Finance
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    ArQuants is a technological solution that facilitates the execution of algorithmic trading through a web platform with access to multiple markets. The company allows stock market operators to optimize the resources used to execute operations, minimize the possibility of human error, enhance investment strategies, and create new business. The interns will carry out research that will help learn more about global algorithm trading. This placement is offered in collaboration with Puentes Abroad.

  • Arquitectura y Paisaje EU

    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Colombia
    Field of Interest: Ecology and Environment, Engineering (ALL), Graphic Design
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    Arquitectura y Paisaje EU is a leading architecture & design studio who has received various national awards and some of the most prestigious international awards in architecture. Interns will work on projects related to urban development, landscape design, environmental sustainability, commercial property and residential property with a strong focus on sustainable and ecological architecture. This placement is offered in collaboration with Intern Colombia.

  • Associao Portuguesa de Apoio Vtima (APAV)

    Region: Europe
    Country: Portugal
    Field of Interest: Community Engagement & Advocacy, Gender and Sexuality
    Type of Organization: Non-profit / NGO

    Associao Portuguesa de Apoio Vtima (APAV) is a private charitable organization, recognized by law with the statutory objective to inform, protect and support citizens (in Europe) who have been victims of crime. It is a non-profit organization assisted by volunteers, which supports victims of crime, in a personal, sensitive and professional way, through the provision of free and confidential services. Founded on 25 June 1990, it has offices nationwide, with headquarters in Lisbon. It collaborates with international institutions such as the European Forum of Restorative Justice, World Society of Victimology, European Network Against Racism and the European Union Agency of Fundamental Rights. Interns will study the technical resource materials, assist in research, and translate

  • Atacama Large Millimeter/submillimeter Array (ALMA)

    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Chile
    Field of Interest: Physical Sciences
    Type of Organization: Academic Institution / Lab Research

    The Atacama Large Millimeter/submillimeter Array (ALMA) is an international partnership of the European Southern Observatory, the U.S. National Science Foundation and the National Institutes of Natural Sciences of Japan, along with organizations in Canada, Taiwan and Korea, in cooperation with the Republic of Chile. ALMA is the largest astronomical project in existence a telescope of revolutionary design, composed of 66 high precision antennas located high on the Chajnantor plateau. Interns will be exposed to this innovative telescope while working with data from ALMA.

  • Athens Voice Media Company

    Region: Europe
    Country: Greece
    Field of Interest: Arts, Culture, and Entertainment, Journalism and Mass Communication
    Type of Organization: For Profit

    Athens Voice is a media company that includes outlets such as the printed ATHENS VOICE newspaper, the digital publication athensvoice.gr, the radio station Athens Voice Radio 102,5 and Athens Voice Books. IIP Interns will be responsible for producing pieces for foreign audiences that travel to Athens in June and July. This placement is offered in collaboration with Empiria Learning.
