Princeton University has established a centralized database to track programs and activities serving Minors. Program administrators are asked to answer basic questions about their program and provide 24-hour contact information.

Is this a Covered or Non-Covered Program?
A Covered Program is one where care, custody and control resides with Princeton faculty, staff or students. A Non-Covered Program is one where the care, custody and control does not reside with a Princeton person and the event is just occurring on campus.
