STEM Education at Cape Cod Community College

Club of Cape Cod

Thursday, June 16, 2022, 6:30PM - 9:30PM

More Info & RSVP

Event Details

Our Cape economy depends almost entirely upon the short tourist season and providing low wage services to a growing retiree population. A healthy robust economy needs to expand into higher value fields. Cape Cod Community College, our local higher education resource, recognizes this. They continue to expand their STEM education offerings, and will shortly be opening a new science and engineering center in support of these offerings.

Dr. John Cox and STEM Dean Dr. Don Crampton will discuss the future of STEM education at the College, the role of this new Frank and Maureen Wilkins Science and Engineering Center, and how they are preparing to serve students and future professionals across the region and beyond.

Book club event - At Night All Blood is Black

Club of Northern New Jersey

Friday, June 17, 2022, 7:00PM - 8:00PM

More Info & RSVP

Event Details

Our next book discussion group meets remotely on June 16 at 7 PM. We will discuss At Night All Blood is Black by David Diop, the 2021 International Booker prize winner that is a deceptively simple but brilliant tale about a Senegalese soldiers descent into madness while fighting for France in the trenches in World War I.  The book has won plenty of awards -- the French Prix Goncourt des Lycens, a Sunday Times best book of the year, and winner of the Los Angeles Times Book Prize for fiction.  And it's only 140 pages!

 

Join us and enrich the discussion. If you have thought or read about war, madness, delusion, colonialism, or racial exploitation, or the literary expressions of these or related themes, you will have something to add.

 

The remote link will be provided to participants a few days before the meeting.
