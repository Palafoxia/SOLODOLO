Policies and Guidelines

Policies You Should Know

Visitor Guidelines

All visitors must be vaccinated and show proof of vaccination as part of visitor attestation.

Travel Restrictions

This policy applies to all travel beginning January 18 and is expected to continue through the summer, subject to public health conditions.

COVID-19 Vaccine Mandate

COVID-19 vaccination is mandatory for all students, faculty, and staff who are present on campus.

Vaccine Booster Mandate

The COVID-19 vaccination mandate is expanded to require booster doses for all faculty, staff, and students who meet eligibility criteria.
