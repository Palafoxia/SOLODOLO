How do I view the student roster in my course?

You will learn how to view the official student roster in Titan Online and how to view an unofficial roster in your course in TITANium.

Decide where you want to view the student roster:

I want to view the official student roster in Titan Online.

Follow these steps to view your official roster in Titan Online.

1. Log in to the CSUF Portal.

2. Click on Titan Online.

2.1. What if I don't see the Titan Online button?
2.1.1. Click on the Hamburger icon in the top left corner.
2.1.2. Type "titan online".
2.1.3. Click on Titan Online when it appears below the search box.

3. Click on the Titan Online button.

4. Click on the Faculty Center link.

The Faculty Center link is located in the Faculty Self Service block. You may need to scroll down the page a little.

5. Click on the Class roster icon.

6. The official student roster for you course will appear.

The official roster in Titan Online shows current enrollments. Adds and Drops are updated immediately each time this report is generated.

I want to view the unofficial student roster in TITANium.

1. Log in to the CSUF Portal.

2. Click on TITANium Faculty.

What if I don't see the TITANIUM FACULTY  button?
2.1. Click on the Hamburger icon in the top left corner.
2.2. Type "titanium".
2.3. Click on TITANium Faculty when it appears below the search box.

3. Click on the TITANium button on the TITANium launch page.

4. Select your course from the list on your Dashboard.

5. Click on Participants.

5.1. What if I don't see the Participants link?
5.1.1. Click on the Hamburger icon to open the Nav drawer.
5.1.2. Click on the Participants link.

6. You can now view a list of the participants in your course.

7. Under Current role, select "Student" from the drop down menu.

8. The list of students in your course is displayed.

Remember that this list of students within your course is not official and includes all users in your course with the role of Student. When a students adds your course, it takes approximately 24 hours to be updated in TITANium. If a student drops your course, it takes at least 72 hours before TITANium is updated.
