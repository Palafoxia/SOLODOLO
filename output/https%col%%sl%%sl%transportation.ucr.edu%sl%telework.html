Learn About Telework

Breadcrumb
  •  
  • Learn About Telework

Telework (working remotely) is supported by Transportation Services and is an accepted travel mode within the R'Commute (alternative transportation) Program. Telework is considered a zero-emission commute mode as it eliminates the need to drive to campus.

Faculty, Staff and Graduate students that telework at least twice per week and do not purchase a monthly, quarterly or annual parking permit are qualified to apply for the R'Commute Program. To verify eligibility, you will need to submit a signed Remote Work Agreement.

Incentives

Registered Telework participants are eligible to receive a complimentary 10-pack parking permit each quarter. Actual allotments provided are dependent on enrollment dates.
