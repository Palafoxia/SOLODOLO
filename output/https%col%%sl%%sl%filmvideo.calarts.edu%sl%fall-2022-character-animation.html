Fall 2022 Class Sign-Up (Part 2)

Character Animation will enroll all optional FVCA and FSFV classes in August, on a date TBA. This will be a unique class sign-up date for Character Animation only.

The classes that will enroll on that date will include Character Design (all levels), Visual Development, Cinematography, Advanced Story, ZBrush, Integrating CG Techniques for Live-Action, Advanced Life Drawing, Acting for Animators, and more.

You'll receive information in advance, and will need to be available on the sign-up date. Please be sure to monitor your email over the summer.
