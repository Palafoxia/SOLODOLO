Centers & Institutes

CSUF is home to a number of Campus Centers and Institutes (CCIs), which address a wide variety of research, service and community needs. The CCIs undergo periodic reviews that serve as a reflective process to summarize the centers/institutes’ accomplishments, and to identify directions of growth and areas of improvement.

  • University Policy Statement (UPS 100.700) on the formation and review of campus centers and institutes
  • Centers and Institutes Establishment Guidelines
