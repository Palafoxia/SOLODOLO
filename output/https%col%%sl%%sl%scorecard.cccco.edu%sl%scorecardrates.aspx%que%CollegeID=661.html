About the college

Established in 1969, College of the Canyons operates within the Santa Clarita Community College District on two campuses in the Santa Clarita Valley. The college offers 62 associate of arts and associate of science degrees and 22 associate degrees for transfer. In addition, the college offers 37 certificate of achievement, 31 certificate of specialization and 5 noncredit certificate programs.

District: Santa Clarita Students: 32,862 County: Los Angeles
