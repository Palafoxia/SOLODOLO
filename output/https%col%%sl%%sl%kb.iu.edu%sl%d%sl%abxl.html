Overview

The UITS Support Center provides IT support to all IU campuses. So that Support Center staff can efficiently assist you, be ready to provide details about your problem or question.

Notes:
  • Walk-in support hours: The hours listed below for walk-in support locations are for periods when classes are in session. Walk-in support hours during campus breaks and between semesters may vary.
  • Research computing support: Research computing support at IU is provided by the Research Technologies division of UITS. To ask a question or get help regarding Research Technologies services, including IU's research supercomputers and research storage systems, and the scientific, statistical, and mathematical applications available on those systems, contact UITS Research Technologies. For service-specific support contact information, see Research computing support at IU.
