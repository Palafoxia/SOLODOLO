College of Business and Economics COVID-19 Update

Our college has gone virtual, but we are still here to support you during these challenging times. The support you need is available online through our virtual support centers.

  • Business Advising Center: Schedule a virtual appointment, email an advisor or submit forms to get in touch with an advisor.
  • Career Services: Schedule a virtual appointment, register for a virtual workshop or reach a career advisor.
  • Tutoring Center: Schedule a virtual tutoring session.
  • Graduate Programs: Graduate students can schedule virtual appointments, email an advisor about their master's program or submit graduate forms.
  • College Departments: Obtain permits or connect with faculty members through the college's six departments: accounting, economics, finance, ISDS, management and marketing.
