How do I get support for McGraw-Hill Connect?

You will learn about ways to get support for McGraw-Hill Connect.

Common Support Questions

Registration Dates

Student's will get a "Registration is not open" error if the registration dates for the course are in the past. If a course was copied from a previous semester, the registration dates need to be updated with the steps in the McGraw-Hill Registration Dates Guide.

Support Resources

McGraw-Hill Support Hub

The McGraw-Hill support web page at the McGraw-Hill Support Page has a variety of support resources.

Contact McGraw-Hill Support

Several options for contacting support are available at Contact Support Site.

CSUF Guides

Instructor guides for using McGraw-Hill Connect in the CSUF Canvas site are available at CSUF McGraw-Hill Website.

Contact CSUF Support

If you need further assistance with MH Connect, please email the details to lmsadmin@fullerton.edu.
