Divisions

Working together with a focus on providing students the resources and support to reach their goals.

The Chancellor’s Office includes nine divisions, each led by vice chancellors who manage policy and supervise employees.

  • College Finance and Facilities Planning

    The College Finance and Facilities Planning Division oversees policies that determine the distribution of local assistance and capital outlay funds for the 73 districts that make up the California Community Colleges. The division also represents the system in advocating for local assistance and capital outlay funds with the Legislature and the Department of Finance.

    Learn More

  • Digital Innovation and Infrastructure

    The Digital Innovation and Infrastructure Division is at the forefront of the future at the California Community Colleges. We are at the cutting edge of improving experiences and outcomes through technology-focused initiatives and delivering high-quality information technology services throughout the college system.

    Learn More

  • Educational Services and Support

    The Educational Services and Support Division is committed to students getting the high-quality curriculum, support and instructional services that they deserve. The division is responsible for providing leadership, oversight and assistance in the following areas:

    Learn More

  • Government Relations

    The Government Relations Division of the California Community Colleges is getting its work done. This division represents the Chancellor’s Office before the legislative and executive branches of the state and federal governments, and it is responsible for advancing the California Community Colleges’ legislative agenda. Recent milestones include basic skills reform, expanded financial aid opportunities, the Student Centered Funding Formula, the California College Promise and the California Online Community College.

    Learn More

  • Institutional Effectiveness

    The Institutional Effectiveness Division is focused on making sure the California Community Colleges is effectively serving its students. It does so largely by overseeing the Institutional Effectiveness Partnership Initiative, commonly referred to as IEPI.

    Learn More

  • Internal Operations

    Welcome to the Internal Operations Division, which prides itself in administering the support services needed to help the Chancellor’s Office pilot the strategic success of California’s community colleges and its students.

    Learn More

  • Office of Communications & Marketing

    Communications is key, and the Office of Communications & Marketing is where you can access news updates, media releases, fact sheets and videos. Our team of communications professionals is charged with developing and maintaining the California Community Colleges’ website with a focus on fostering success among current and prospective students.

    Learn More

  • Office of General Counsel

    Provide legal counsel to the Board of Governors of the California Community Colleges, the chancellor and Chancellor’s Office staff as requested.

    Learn More

  • Workforce and Economic Development

    Our community colleges are the leading provider of workforce training in the world and we are uniquely positioned to improve the social and economic mobility of Californians from all backgrounds.

    Learn More
