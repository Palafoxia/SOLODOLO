DPS Divisions

The Department of Public Safety operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, and is composed of 12 different divisions that help us to maintain our excellent level of service. These committed men and women contribute to a comprehensive and integrated safety and security program in collaboration with the Princeton Community.
