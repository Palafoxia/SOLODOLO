Faculty & Industry Experts

As a Master of Science in Human Resource Management student, you will be taught by a dedicated and practiced group of core faculty and industry leaders who are driving the future of this thriving and exciting field.

You will also benefit from a cadre of collaborators who have contributed their unique expertise to

  • create a student-centered, tech-savvy and supportive learning environment
  • ensure the curriculum is at the leading edge of the field
  • provide access to some of the top minds in human resources management
  • offer career coaching, with tailored advice and resources to help you achieve your goals

In addition to offering ongoing support for the program, our advisory committee of top human resources executives provided key input that infused the curriculum and its learning outcomes with the skills most sought-after by employers.

These HR innovators provide ongoing support for the Master of Science in Human Resource Management to help ensure the degree and our students and graduates remain at the cutting edge of the field.
