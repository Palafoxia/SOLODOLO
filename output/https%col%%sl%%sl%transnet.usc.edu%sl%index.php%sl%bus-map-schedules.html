Bus Schedules

Summer Semester 2022 Routes begin Monday, May 16th

Hello Trojans! Our Summer Semester 2022 bus routes begin Monday, May 16th. Here are the changes that will take effect:

  • SC Tower (City Center) will move to University Club. ( on 34th & Trousdale)
  • C-Route runs every 30 minutes from 6:30am-6:00pm. Now services 34th street, no longer services USC Village.
  • Marina Del Rey will move to Vivian Hall (on Watt Way)
  • The Parking Center route has been moved back on campus and the stops consist of:
    1. Grand Structure
    2. Carol Little Building(CAL)-North
    3. Leavey Library
    4. JEP House
    5. Dental School
    6. 34th & McClintock
    7. Michelson
    8. Pardee Way Entrance
    9. Transit Center (7am-6pm)
    10. Research Annex (RAN) (7am-6pm)
    11. Carol Little Building(CAL)-South (7am-6pm)
Important Reminders:
  • Face Masks are required on all buses.
  • At the entrance door of every bus, we have a hand-sanitizer station for our passengers to utilize.
  • For the safety of our drivers, we have a clear plastic curtain that separates our drivers from the rear cabin.
  • For all buses with 2 doors, boarding is only allowed using the rear door.
