A Trojan Check is no longer required for campus access. Masks are recommended but not required, except in some indoor settings such as health care facilities. Students, faculty and staff who are not up to date on vaccines are required to test once per week. Read the latest memo. Stay informed on USC’s current plans by visiting our information website.
