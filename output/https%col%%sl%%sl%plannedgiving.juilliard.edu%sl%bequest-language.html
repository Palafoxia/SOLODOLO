Bequest Language

To establish an unrestricted gift in your estate plan for Juilliard we would suggest language like:

"I give and bequeath the sum of $_____________ (or _____% of the rest, residue and remainder of my estate) unto The Juilliard School, New York, New York, for the general support of the School."

To establish a scholarship through your estate plan, we would suggest using language like the following:

"I give and bequeath the sum of $_____________ (or _____% of my residual estate) unto The Juilliard School, New York, New York. I direct that this gift shall establish the ______________ Scholarship (whatever name you prefer), to provide assistance to deserving students, with a first preference for those studying (restriction, if any). I further request that, should this fund meet the financial requirement, the School permanently endow this scholarship."
