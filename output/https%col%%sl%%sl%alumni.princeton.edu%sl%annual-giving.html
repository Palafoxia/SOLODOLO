Annual Giving

Image

Unrestricted gifts from undergraduate and graduate alumni, parents, and friends help provide the margin of excellence that makes a Princeton education second to none. Annual Giving sustains and enhances the Universitys distinctive academic programs and brings together Princetonians of all generations.
