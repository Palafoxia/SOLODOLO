New Student Advising and Registration

 

Welcome College of the Arts Students,
 
We are excited that you will begin your academic journey this Fall 2022 semester in the College of the Arts at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF). It is a destination of choice for students seeking the highest quality training in the visual and performing arts in an environment that encourages individual achievement and artistic expression. The College of the Arts has a strong commitment to excellence. Our vibrant learning community of faculty, administrators, and staff is committed to helping students achieve their educational goals.
 
Newly admitted students will be contacted by their respective department to schedule an advising appointment. Please check your campus email account daily.  
 
Congratulations again on your CSUF admission, we look forward to sharing the next few years of excellence with you.
