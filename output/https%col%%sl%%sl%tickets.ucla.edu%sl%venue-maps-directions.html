Parking

Central Ticket Office (Parking Structure #4 or #8)
The Central Ticket Office is located in the James West Alumni Center, just east of Pauley Pavilion. To visit our office, please follow the directions below and use the Campus Map for reference.

If you plan to only be on campus for a short period of time, it is recommended that you park in one of the "Self-Service Parking Pay Stations" located in both Structure 4 (entering campus off Sunset Blvd) and Structure 8 (entering campus off Wilshire Blvd). 

.

Directions

From I-405 (North & South)
Exit Wilshire Boulevard heading east. Turn left onto Westwood Boulevard and continue traveling north to enter campus. Parking Structure #8 is on your left. 

From Sunset Blvd
Exit Sunset Boulevard heading east. Turn right Westwood Boulevard and continue into Parking Structure #4 and turn left into the "Self-Service Pay Stations". 

From Wilshire Blvd
Turn north onto Westwood Boulevard and continue traveling north to enter campus. Parking Structure #8 is on your left.
