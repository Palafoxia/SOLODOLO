January 2021

  • January 26

    Winter Quarter Census Date

    Students enrolled less than full-time (12 units) on census date (one day after the last day to add classes on GOLD) will have their Cal Grant and Pell Grant for that quarter reduced to a level that reflects their enrollment level. Students enrolled in less than 6 units on this date will have their UCSB Scholarship reduced in its entirety. 

    More information can be found here: https://www.finaid.ucsb.edu/grants
