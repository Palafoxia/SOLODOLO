Study Abroad Opportunities

Learn about program types.

  • 3 Campus East Asian Studies Program

    Term: Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences
    Region: Asia
    Country: Hong Kong, Japan, South Korea
    Language of Instruction: English, Korean
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Amanda El-Kadi

    Princeton students can participate in an exciting three-country program that will take them to China, Japan, and Korea. Students will be among a cohort from select US and European universities studying at Keio University, Yonsei University, and the University of Hong Kong where they will experience East Asia from a genuinely comparative perspective.

  • Academic Programs Abroad

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: France
    Language of Instruction: French
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    APA offers American students the opportunity to discover a new culture by immersing themselves in French academic, cultural and social life. To ensure you a truly excellent academic experience, APA partners with some of the most prestigious and reputable universities in Paris, and students can take courses at multiple universities throughout the city.

  • AMIDEAST Area and Arabic Language Studies in Jordan

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences
    Region: Middle East
    Country: Jordan
    Language of Instruction: Arabic
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    Students on the Area & Arabic Language Studies program in Amman have the opportunity to study both Modern Standard Arabic and Jordanian Arabic, in addition to electives taught in English, and learn first-hand from leading faculty from the Qasid Arabic Institute about the critically important Middle East region.

  • AMIDEAST Area and Arabic Language Studies in Morocco

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences
    Region: Africa
    Country: Morocco
    Language of Instruction: Arabic
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    AMIDEAST Morocco is a great fit for students looking to improve their Arabic language skills by taking one Modern Standard Arabic course and one Moroccan dialect course, in addition to content-courses in English. Students with the equivalent of four semesters of college-level French also have the opportunity to take courses in French.

  • Aquincum Institute of Technology (AIT Budapest)

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Engineering (BSE), STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: Hungary
    Language of Instruction: English
    Language Prerequisite: No
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    For students interested in computer science, creative design, or IT entrepreneurship, AIT offers the opportunity to take courses in English in the beautiful city of Budapest, taught by local Hungarian professors and globally-acclaimed scholars and entrepreneurs. Students also have the opportunity to take courses on Hungarian language and culture.

  • Associated Colleges in China

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Humanities, Social Sciences
    Region: Asia
    Country: China
    Language of Instruction: Chinese
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Direct enrollment
    Adviser: Amanda El-Kadi

    Hamiltons Associated Colleges in China, based in Beijing, is among the top Chinese language programs where participants pledge to speak only Chinese. Students gain advanced language proficiency, make the most of cultural opportunities and develop an understanding of Chinas people, economy and educational system.

  • Berlin Consortium for German Studies

    Term: Academic Year, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: Germany
    Language of Instruction: German
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    This program is designed for students with an advanced level of German to take courses directly enrolled at Freie Universitat Berlin (FU Berlin). Students take an intensive German course at the beginning of the semester, and then will register for university courses in areas such as the humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and more.

  • Bocconi University (Exchange)

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Social Sciences
    Region: Europe
    Country: Italy
    Language of Instruction: English, Italian
    Language Prerequisite: No
    Program Type: Direct enrollment, Princeton exchange program
    Adviser: Gisella Gisolo

    Princeton students concentrating or planning to concentrate in economics have the opportunity to take courses (in English or Italian) at one of the foremost economics and finance universities in Europe. Bocconi University is a small, selective institution located near Milan's financial district and stock exchange, surrounded by a bustling and cosmopolitan city life.

  • Boston University London Art History Program with the Courtauld Institute of Art

    Term: Fall
    Academic Interest Area: Arts
    Region: Europe
    Country: United Kingdom
    Language of Instruction: English
    Language Prerequisite: No
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Amanda El-Kadi

    The London Art History Program with the Courtauld Institute of Art offers something truly unique to serious students of art: a semester of art history at Britain's preeminent art institute, the world-renowned Courtauld Institute of Art at the University of London.

  • Boston University Madrid University Studies

    Term: Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: Spain
    Language of Instruction: Spanish
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    This program is a great fit for those students who already at an advanced level of Spanish language and are interested in taking a full load of content courses in Spanish, in areas such as history, politics, anthropology and more. Students take courses at the Universidad Autonoma de Madrid alongside other domestic and international students, in addition to one Contemporary Spanish History course taught through BU.

  • Boston University Padua Italian Studies Program

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences
    Region: Europe
    Country: Italy
    Language of Instruction: Italian
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Gisella Gisolo

    The BU Padua Italian Studies Program is located in a lively Northeastern Italian town that is home to one of the oldest universities in the world. Students will focus on Italian language, history, art, and culture by taking courses taught in Italian at the Boston University Padua Center and at the University of Padua.

  • Brown in Brazil: Consortium for Advanced Studies Abroad (CASA)

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Latin America / Caribbean
    Country: Brazil
    Language of Instruction: Portuguese
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    Brown in Brazil offers students the opportunity to immerse themselves completely in the culture and language of Brazil through intensive language instruction and direct enroll courses at Pontificia Universidade Catolica in the areas of politics, history, literature, art history, and more. Designed for students with an intermediate level of Portuguese.

  • Brown in Italy, University of Bologna

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: Italy
    Language of Instruction: Italian
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Gisella Gisolo

    Through the Brown in Bologna program, students can study at the oldest university in Europe in a city with great cultural, historic and culinary traditions. In addition to the mandatory pre-seminar focusing on Italian language and culture, students will take direct-enroll courses (all in Italian) at the University of Bologna and live with other students in Brown-owned apartments.

  • Budapest Semesters in Mathematics

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: Hungary
    Language of Instruction: English
    Language Prerequisite: No
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    BSM offers students the opportunity to take an intensive semester of mathematics at the College International in Budapest alongside other North American students. Courses are taught in English and taught by eminent Hungarian professors, all while living in the beautiful and historic city of Budapest.

  • Carleton Global Engagement Program: Buddhist Studies

    Term: Fall
    Academic Interest Area: Humanities, Social Sciences
    Region: Asia
    Country: India
    Language of Instruction: English, Hindi
    Language Prerequisite: No
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    The Carleton Global Engagement Program is a unique opportunity for students that allows them to study Buddhism while living in a monastery and experiencing an engaging and supportive environment. Students have the opportunity to take Hindi language and also conduct an independent study on a topic related to Buddhist Studies.

  • Center for University Programs Abroad (CUPA)

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: France
    Language of Instruction: French
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    CUPA provides personalized methodology, language mentoring, and extensive academic, cultural and personal resources for students with advanced French language skills in Paris. Students will take courses across several well-renowned French universities, and the language pledge and homestay experience will allow students for deeper growth both in their language skills and cultural competency.

  • CET Intensive Arabic Language in Jordan

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Humanities, Social Sciences
    Region: Middle East
    Country: Jordan
    Language of Instruction: Arabic
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    For those looking for a full immersion, Arabic language program, look no further than CET in Jordan. Take a full course-load of Arabic courses, two language courses and two content courses, and live alongside other Jordanian students in a residence hall.

  • Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Engineering (BSE), Humanities, Pre-health, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Asia
    Country: China
    Language of Instruction: Chinese, English
    Language Prerequisite: No
    Program Type: Direct enrollment
    Adviser: Amanda El-Kadi

    The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) is a top comprehensive research university in a trilingual campus (English, Chinese, Cantonese). Students participating in this program may take courses by a range of departments as well as by the International Asian Studies Program and the Yale-China Chinese Language Center.

  • CIEE Advanced Liberal Arts Program in Barcelona, Spain

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: Spain
    Language of Instruction: Spanish
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    CIEE Barcelona is designed for students at an intermediate level of Spanish who are eager to take their language skills to the next level by taking courses at Universitat de Barcelona alongside other Spanish and international students. In addition to courses in the Humanities and Social Sciences, Universitat Barcelona also offers courses in STEM areas of biology, chemistry, physics, and more.

  • CIEE Advanced Liberal Arts Program in Seville, Spain

    Term: Academic Year, Fall, Spring
    Academic Interest Area: Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, STEM (AB)
    Region: Europe
    Country: Spain
    Language of Instruction: Spanish
    Language Prerequisite: Yes
    Program Type: Third-party provider
    Adviser: Jordan Zilla

    CIEE Advanced Liberal Arts is an excellent fit for those students who are already at an intermediate to advanced level of Spanish language and who are interested in taking a mix of university and program-taught courses. Students have the opportunity to study and live in the beautiful city of Seville, the commercial hub of the Andaluz region with a vibrant history dating back to the 8th century BC.
