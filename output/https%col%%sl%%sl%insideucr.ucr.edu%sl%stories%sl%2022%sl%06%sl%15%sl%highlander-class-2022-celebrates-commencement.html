Highlander Class of 2022 celebrates commencement

See photos from commencement ceremonies

June 15, 2022
Author:University Communications
June 15, 2022

UC Riverside held 12 commencement ceremonies over the past week, attracting thousands of graduates and their families in the biggest in-person graduation celebration on campus since before the pandemic.

The ceremonies began Friday, June 3, and concluded Monday, June 13. A total of 7,272 students were eligible to graduate. That included 1,117 students in the School of Business, 420 in the School of Education, 1,006 in the Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering, 2,808 in the College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences, 1,682 in the College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences, 81 in the School of Medicine and 158 in the School of Public Policy.
