Add Feed to your Calendar

To add the events calendar to your google calendar:

  • At the home page, at the bottom, click the “iCal Feed” button.
  • Copy the iCal feed URL
  • In your google calendar, in the Other Calendars area, click on the “+”
  • Choose “From URL”
  • Paste the URL, click “Add to Calendar”
