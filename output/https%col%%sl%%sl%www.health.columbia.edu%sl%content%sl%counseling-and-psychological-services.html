    By Phone

    If you are experiencing high levels of distress and are in need of immediate assistance, please call 212-854-2878, available 24/7.

    Urgent Mental Health Concerns

    Walk-in session if you are experiencing high levels of distress.

    Closed during Summer term.

    Problem-Solving Drop-in

    Brief session to talk about an everyday life concern impacting emotional well-being.

    Closed during Summer term.

    Podcast

    Our limited podcast series, Invisible and On Stage, provides an open conversation around racism-related stress, identity, and community, and the way they impact mental health. Listen now!

    In the 2020-2021 academic year:

    Counseling and Psychological Services saw 5,148 students for individual counseling, provided 1,723 consultations for faculty, administrators, parents, and other members of the community concerned about a student, and offered 111 virtual support spaces.
